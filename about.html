Who We Are

A trusted leather goods manufacturing and export company proudly operating from Kanpur, India's renowned leather hub.

Kingsman Leather is dedicated to delivering premium quality leather products for domestic and international markets. Our focus is on combining traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing techniques to produce durable, elegant and commercially successful leather goods.

We work closely with wholesalers, retailers, distributors, corporate buyers and private label brands seeking reliable manufacturing partners in India.

Whether you require custom-branded wallets, executive gifting solutions or large-scale export production, Kingsman Leather is committed to quality, consistency and long-term partnerships.
